Spaghetti

Description

A savoury meal that finishes off any good night! Have it with meatballs or simply by itself with sauce! Spaghetti is the perfect meal for you to enjoy when you are hungry at night and don't know what to eat.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Meatballs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put water to a boil.
  2. Boil spaghetti for 6-7 minutes until al dente.
  3. Strain the pasta and leave some pasta water for the sauce.
  4. Simmer marinara sauce in a saucepan.
  5. Place meatballs in simmering sauce.
  6. After simmering sauce, pour some of the pasta water into the sauce.
  7. Mix well then place pasta into the sauce.
